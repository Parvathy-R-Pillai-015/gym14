## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Can I change my fitness goal later?**
A: Yes! Go to your profile and update your goal. Your diet plan and workout videos will automatically adjust.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter your email, and follow the OTP verification process.

**Q: Can I have multiple trainers?**
A: No, you're assigned one dedicated trainer based on your goal. However, you can request a change through admin.

### Diet Plan Questions

**Q: Can I modify my diet plan?**
A: You can discuss modifications with your trainer. They can customize your plan based on preferences and restrictions.

**Q: What if I'm allergic to certain foods?**
A: Inform your trainer immediately. They will create a customized plan avoiding those foods.

**Q: How often does my diet plan change?**
A: Typically every 7-30 days, depending on your progress. Your trainer monitors and adjusts as needed.

### Payment Questions

**Q: What if I forget my payment PIN?**
A: Contact admin to reset your PIN. You'll need to verify your identity and set a new PIN.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policy depends on gym terms. Contact admin for specific situations.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, and UPI are supported for online payments.

**Q: When should I renew my subscription?**
A: You can renew up to 7 days before expiry. You'll receive notifications when renewal is due.

### Workout Questions

**Q: How many videos should I watch per day?**
A: Follow your trainer's guidance. Typically 1-2 videos per workout session.

**Q: Can I do advanced videos as a beginner?**
A: Start with beginner videos to master basics and avoid injury. Progress to advanced when ready.

**Q: What if I can't perform an exercise?**
A: Chat with your trainer for modifications or alternative exercises.

### Attendance Questions

**Q: What if my trainer doesn't approve my attendance?**
A: Contact your trainer directly. There may be confusion about dates or they may not have seen you at gym.

**Q: Can I mark attendance for past dates?**
A: No, attendance can only be marked for the current date to maintain accuracy.

**Q: Does attendance affect my subscription?**
A: No, but tracking attendance helps you and your trainer monitor consistency.
